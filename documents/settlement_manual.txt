Trade Settlement Process

A trade settlement is the process of transferring securities from the seller to the buyer.

Settlement Types

T+1
Trade settles next business day.

T+2
Trade settles after two business days.

T+3
Trade settles after three business days.

Products

Equity
Represents ownership in a company.

Bond
Debt instrument issued by governments or companies.

ETF
Exchange Traded Fund.

Derivative
Financial contract based on another asset.

Priorities

High
Requires immediate settlement.

Medium
Standard processing.

Low
Can be processed later.
